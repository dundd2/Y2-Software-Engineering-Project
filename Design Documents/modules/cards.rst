Cards Module
============

This module handles the "Pot Luck" and "Opportunity Knocks" cards used in the game. It defines the `Card` class to represent individual cards with their text and actions, the `CardType` enum, and the `CardDeck` class to manage shuffling, drawing, and discarding cards.
