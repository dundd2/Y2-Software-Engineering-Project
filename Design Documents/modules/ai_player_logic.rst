AI Player Logic Module
======================

This module contains the logic for Artificial Intelligence (AI) players. It defines different AI difficulty levels (`EasyAIPlayer`, `HardAIPlayer`) with varying strategies for decision-making, such as buying properties, participating in auctions, developing properties, and handling jail situations. The `HardAIPlayer` includes a mood system influencing its decisions.
